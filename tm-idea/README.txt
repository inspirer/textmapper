Textmapper - IntelliJ IDEA plug-in

Manual installation
===================

1. Download and unpack archive into your plug-ins folder:

    Linux/Windows: <USER_HOME>/.IdeaIC2016.2/config/plugins
    OS X: <USER_HOME>/Library/Application Support/IdeaIC2016.2

2. (Re)start IntelliJ IDEA
3. Check that plugin is installed: Settings -> Plugins -> Installed -> Textmapper
